THE DEEP SEA

Sunlight

Also called the epipelagic or euphotic zone, this level of water extends 200 meters below the surface. Sunlight is able to penetrate this layer, allowing for heating and temperature variation. The sunlight zone is very small compared to the other zones.

ZONE OCEAN DEPTH
Sunlight zone Surface to 200m
Twlight zone 200m to 1000m
Midnight zone 1000m to 4000m
Abyssal zone 4000m to 6000m
Hadal zone 6000m to 10,994m

What lives here?

Thanks to the sunlight, photosynthesis may occur here. Innumerable marine species live in this level.

Tuna...

are among the many fish that live in the sunlight zone. Their bodies are built for incredibly fast speeds.


Coral...

are actually animals, not plants, so they do not photosynthesize. Algae grows on coral, which benefits them.
