CETUS

Known as the Sea Monster, or the Whale. It is the 4th largest constellation in the sky.

In mythology, the hero Perseus slayed Cetus (who did not look like a whale) to save the princess Andromeda, who was chained to a rock as a sacrifice.

DRACO

The dragon, the 8th largest constellation in the sky.

Draco can be found in numerous myths, but is most often Ladon of Hercules' 12 labors. Ladon guarded Hera's golden apple tree, which Hercules had to steal from.

URSA MAJOR

"The Great Bear" is the largest constellation in the northern hemisphere.

One story of Ursa Major revolves around Callisto, who was turned into the bear by Hera. This prominent constellation has many different interpretations and stories from around the world.
